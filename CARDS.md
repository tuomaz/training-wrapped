# Training Wrapped 2025 - Cards

This document lists the slides currently implemented in the Training Wrapped application.

## 1. Intro
- **Heading:** 2025 TRAINING WRAPPED
- **Visuals:** Purple-to-black gradient, animated pulse text.
- **Action:** Instructions to tap or use arrow keys.

## 2. The Big Numbers (The Grind)
- **Heading:** The Grind
- **Stats:**
    - **Total Sessions:** Aggregate count of all training activities.
    - **Hours Sweating:** Total duration in hours.
- **Icons:** Activity (green), Clock (yellow).

## 3. Consistency & Streak (Unstoppable)
- **Heading:** Unstoppable
- **Stats:**
    - **Day Streak:** Longest consecutive run of days with activity.
- **Visuals:** Orange flame icon with bounce animation.

## 4. Monthly Breakdown (Year in Review)
- **Heading:** Year in Review
- **Visuals:** Stacked bar chart showing Running, Cycling, and Gym sessions per month.
- **Colors:** Running (Cyan), Cycling (Green), Gym (Pink).

## 5. Peak Performance
- **Heading:** Peak Performance
- **Stats:**
    - **Best Run Week:** Distance (km) and week number.
    - **Best Bike Week:** Distance (km) and week number.
    - **Best Gym Month:** Number of sessions and month name.
- **Icons:** Zap (Cyan), Bike (Green), Dumbbell (Pink).

## 6. Gym Stats (Iron Paradise)
- **Heading:** Iron Paradise
- **Stats:**
    - **Total Gym Sessions:** Count of gym-specific entries.
    - **Most Frequent Lifts:** Top 3 exercises parsed from the gym log.
- **Visuals:** Large pink dumbbell icon.

## 7. Cardio Stats (On The Move)
- **Heading:** On The Move
- **Stats:**
    - **Longest Run:** Single longest distance (km).
    - **Longest Ride:** Single longest distance (km).
    - **Total Kilometers:** Combined distance for all cardio.
- **Visuals:** Footprints and Bike icons, gradient background.

## 8. Running Hall of Fame
- **Heading:** Running Hall of Fame
- **Stats:**
    - **Top 5 Distance:** List of dates and distances for the longest runs.
    - **Top 5 Speed:** List of dates and paces (min/km) for the fastest runs.
- **Visuals:** Two columns with cyan and yellow accents, Footprints and Zap icons.

## 9. Cycling Hall of Fame
- **Heading:** Cycling Hall of Fame
- **Stats:**
    - **Top 5 Distance:** List of dates and distances for the longest rides.
    - **Top 5 Speed:** List of dates and speeds (km/h) for the fastest rides. Uses ODS speed data when available.
- **Visuals:** Two columns with green and yellow accents, Bike and Zap icons.

## 10. Outro
- **Heading:** You Crushed It!
- **Text:** "See you in 2026."
- **Insight:** "Power days" based on the most active day of the week.
- **Visuals:** Bouncing trophy icon.
